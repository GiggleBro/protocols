VocAra Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 30, 2025

Welcome to VocAra ("the App"). This app is suitable for users aged 4 and above. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. Please read it carefully before using the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, crash logs.
  • Account Information (if applicable): Email address, username.
  • Content Data: Audio files uploaded for transcription and translation.
  • Generated Content: Transcribed and translated texts.
  • Usage Data: Feature usage frequency, interaction preferences.
  • Payment Information: Handled by Apple/Google; we do not store your payment data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide transcription and translation services.
  • To improve features and user experience.
  • To enhance AI model accuracy.
  • To fix bugs and improve stability.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

  • We do not sell or rent your data to third parties unless:
  • Required by law or regulation.
  • In case of merger, acquisition, or asset transfer.
  • We use third-party AI services (data anonymized).
  • We collect anonymous usage analytics.

4. Your Rights

  • Access, modify, or delete your personal data (if applicable).
  • Disable specific permissions in device settings.
  • Clear history and generated content in the app.
  • Unsubscribe via iOS/Android system settings.

5. Data Security

  • Uploaded audio is used for immediate processing only.
  • We use encryption for transmitting sensitive information.
  • We conduct regular security audits.

6. Notes for Minors

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
  • AI content is automatically filtered for age appropriateness.
  • Parental controls can be set via device settings.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted here. Continuing to use the app indicates your acceptance of changes.

8. Disclaimer

AI-generated content is for reference only. Accuracy or applicability is not guaranteed.
