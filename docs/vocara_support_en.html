VocAra Technical Support

Thank you for using VocAra! We are committed to providing a high-quality voice-to-text and translation experience. If you encounter any issues, please refer to the FAQs below or contact our support team.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Can't upload audio?
    A: Please check your internet connection and ensure the file is in a supported format (m4a/mp3/wav).
  • Q: Transcription failed or inaccurate?
    A: Ensure the recording is clear and in a quiet environment. If the issue persists, try re-uploading.
  • Q: Translation seems incorrect?
    A: VocAra uses AI models for translation, which may result in occasional inaccuracies. Contact us for serious issues.
